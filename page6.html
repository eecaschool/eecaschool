Admission Procedure

1

Obtain the Application Form from the School Office.

2

Complete and return the application form with fees as indicated, Medical history, previous report card, copy of birth certificate and a recent passport photograph

3

A diagnostic test would be taken for children in Reading Readiness (Nursery 2) and above.  

4

Initial interview for both parents and students would be scheduled. 

5

After acceptance, complete the registration and payment of all necessary fees.

6

Complete the Parent orientation the Saturday before school term begins.
