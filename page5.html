OUR VISION

To raise Godly children who will serve God in integrity and righteousness - children who will pursue and achieve all round excellence. All round excellence for us it means excelling in both academics and in the pursuit of heaven (outliving the physical).

Our Mission 

 Provide Biblically based individualized holistic education
 Develop Godly character
 Train children to be useful to God’s Kingdom and humanity
 Be a standard of reformed education.

Eternal excellence Christian academy (EECA) came to being as a result of an inspiration from Isaiah 60:15b…………’ I will make you an eternal excellence, a joy to generations’. We understand that God wants our children to be the change agents this world needs and to be sources of joy to us and to generations yet unborn.

We are committed to the highest standard of academic excellence and character development that hinge on God’s word, strengthening the lives of students and leading to the transformation of society.

EECA is unique, as its approach is not conventional. It places emphasis on learning rather than teaching and a great deal is done to recognize and motivate individual pupils. Learning is the child’s responsibility. Very close co-operation with parents is encouraged and expected to achieve the needed success.

The purpose of the School is to provide biblically based individualized holistic Education, with a view to developing character, reforming education, and training children to be useful to God’s kingdom and His humanity.
Christian education is not an alternative. It is not a luxury. It is not even just a good idea. It is the law of God. It is the law that He gave to our forefathers, and it is the same law that He now gives to us. It is the GREAT COMMANDMENT!
